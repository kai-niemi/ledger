Welcome to Ledger

A financial accounting ledger based on the double-entry bookkeeping principle.

Ledger manages monetary accounts and a journal of balanced, multi-legged transfers between those accounts. Its designed to showcase CockroachDB's scalability, survival, consistency and data domiciling capabilities.

Scalability

A distributed system that feels like a single, logical database. Scale reads, writes, and database capacity by simply adding nodes.

Survival

Continues to serve queries even when nodes, availability zones, and even entire regions fail and when performing maintenance.

Consistency

Every machine can serve both reads and writes for the entire keyspace and provides ACID compliant transactions without compromising consistency during failure or contention.

Locality

Pin data to regions closest to end users to reduce latency and address placement compliance regulations with a multi-region deployment.
